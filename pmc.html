Services

PMC offers both traditional and contemporary services. At 9:00 am there is a traditional service and at 11:45 am the second service starts with a more contemporary service. They also have sabbath school with classes for children from craddle roll till young adults.

Sabbath School: 10:30 am Traditional Service: 9:00 am Contemporary Service: 11:45 am

AU Wind Symphony Winter Concert

February 8, 8 PM—Howard Performing Arts Center
The AU Wind Symphony, under the direction of Alan Mitchell, will present their annual Winter Concert. The first half of the concert will feature the Southwestern Michigan College Brass Band under the direction of Dr. Jonathan Korzun. The brass band will perform pieces by Vaughn Williams, Foster, and more. The AU Wind Symphony will play highlights from popular film scores, among other pieces. Tickets may be purchased at the door.
